Vagamon

It is the place where the heart finds solace amidst the soothing hills.(visited on 18.09.2022)

Some special facts about Vagamon...

Located in the Western Ghats 25 kilometres (16 mi) east of Erathupetta on the border of Kottayam-Idukki districts, Vagamon is famous for its natural beauty. The Vagamon glass bridge is the longest cantilever glass bridge in India. It is a beautiful place to visit during the winter season and one of the best place for honeymoon trip and family trip.

Dhanushkodi

The place from where the Ram Setu starts.(visited on 31.12.2022)

Some special facts about Dhanushkodi..

If myths are to be believed, a particularly beautiful stretch of sandy shore you see from Dhanushkodi is the place from where the Ram Setu starts. This is also believed to be the end of the bridge which Lord Rama had broken using his bow and arrow.

4 Must-Visit places in Dhanushkodi

  • Dhanushkodi beach.
  • Gulf of Mannae Marine National Park
  • Adam's Bridge.
  • Pamban's Island.
